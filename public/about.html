About ARRAutomation

Hi, We are Arman & Rohit – your QA & HR Automation partner.

We work with small businesses and early-stage startups to design simple, practical systems for HR automation and QA testing. Instead of forcing you to buy heavy tools, we focus on lightweight solutions that your team can actually use.

Our job is to help you stop losing time in spreadsheets, manual checks and inconsistent processes. Whether it’s payroll and attendance or testing your core product flows, I help you create structure, clarity and peace of mind.
